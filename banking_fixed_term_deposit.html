How Do I Approach Opening A Savings Bank Account?
You Can Just Walk Into Our Branch To Open An Account
How Can I Update My Mobile Number?
Fill Up Customer Request Form, Mention The Mobile Number & Submit It To The Nearest Branch.
At What Frequency Does The Interest Have To Be Paid?
Interest Will Be Calculated On A Daily Basis And Accrued Interest Will Be Credited Into The Account Quaterly.
Whom I Can Include As A Joint Applicant?
Anyone Can Be Included As A Joint Applicant except A Minor.
How Can I Change The Nominee In The Account?
The Nominee Can Be Changed By The Account Holder, By Making The Declaration In The Nomination Form.
How To Update My New Address In The Account?
Any one of the following documents can be submitted along with the request to change the address

Valid passport
Voter ID
Electricity bill (not older than 2 months)
Driving licence
Landline telephone bill
Any other valid proof issued by a government body
