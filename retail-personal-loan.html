Frequently Asked Questions

How can one open a Current Account?
You Can Just Walk Into Our Branch To Open An Account
Who can open the current account?
The following can open a current account:

Any individual or group of individuals can open a current account
Sole proprietorship Firm
Partnership firm
Public/Private limited company
Trust, association, society, club
Is the Nomination facility available?
Yes, nomination facility is available only in the case of individual current accounts and proprietorship firms
How much cash can be withdrawn from the current account?
There is no cash withdrawal limit. But for large amount of 2 lakhs & above, advance information has to be given to the branch of the bank.
Can the debit card facility be availed?
Yes, only in case of individual and proprietorship firms
