Frequently Asked Questions

Which are the securities against which Secured Loan/Overdraft can be availed from Bank?
You may take Loan/Overdraft against Fixed deposit, NSC, LIC and KVP.
What are the tenure options for Loan/Overdraft against Fixed deposit/KVP/NSC/LIC?
You can repay your loan over a period of 12 to 60 months depending upon the maturity of the securities. Overdraft is a running account.
How can I repay my Loan/Overdraft against Fixed deposit/KVP/NSC/LIC?
The loan can be paid through ECS channel. Further, you can also pay through a standing instruction by debit to your Subhadra Bank account as per your convenience, however on the maturity date of securities the amount t outstanding would be recovered in full from the maturity proceeds. In case of overdraft as it is a running account there would be continuous debits/credits and the overdraft facility can be fully repaid from the maturity proceeds of the securities. In case the overdraft facility is further required substitute, securities can be given to the Bank.
Can I foreclose or prepay my Loan?
You can foreclose/prepay the entire loan outstanding anytime.
Do I need to open an account with Subhadra Bank for availing and / or servicing the loan/overdraft?
We will encourage you to start a Banking relationship with us to get the opportunity of other value added services being offered time to time on respective products. Opening an account with us is mandatory.

MSMES

Variety of Loans with attractive interest of rate and flexible payback options to individual and group of individuals.

We offer loans to corporates and traders to meet their working capital requirements.

We offer various categories of loans to meet business requirements of the traders.

MSMEs

Business Loan
Subhadra Bank is committed to provide funding to small businesses to meet their day to day financial needs to better run their businesses. This is a loan which can be repaid in easy instalments available at a very competitive rate.
Business Loan (100 Days’ Repayment Scheme)
This is a unique product designed by Subhadra Bank exclusively for small business, whereby one can repay entire loan in 100 days with 100 daily instalments. These are suitable for very small businesses operational in rural and semi urban areas.
Cash Credit/ Working Capital funding
This credit facility helps small businesses to bridge the funding gap and enhance business capabilities by adding to their day to day working capital requirements in terms of raw material, stocks, etc.
